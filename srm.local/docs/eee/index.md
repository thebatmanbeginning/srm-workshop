# B.Tech. Electrical & Electronics Engineering

## Welcome to EEE Program at SRMIST

The B.Tech. (Electrical and Electronics Engineering) programme, established in 1992 under the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the Kattankulathur Campus, is accredited by the **Engineering Accreditation Commission (EAC) of ABET** ([https://www.abet.org](https://www.abet.org)). The programme also holds accreditation from the **National Board of Accreditation (NBA)**, India, underscoring its commitment to quality and global academic standards.

## Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

### PEO 1: Technical Excellence
Graduates are in a position to apply their knowledge acquired in Mathematics, Basic Sciences and Electrical and Electronics Engineering courses, to the solutions of complex problems encountered in the modern Engineering practice.

### PEO 2: Continuous Learning
Graduates learn and adapt themselves to the constantly evolving technology by pursuing higher studies.

### PEO 3: Professional Success
Graduates are better employable and achieve success in their chosen areas of Electrical and Electronics Engineering and related fields.

### PEO 4: Leadership & Communication
Graduates are good leaders and managers by effectively communicating at both technical and interpersonal levels.
