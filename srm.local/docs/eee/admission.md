# Admission Process

## How to Apply for B.Tech EEE Program

The admission process at SRMIST is designed to be simple and student-friendly. Follow these three steps to secure your seat in the B.Tech Electrical & Electronics Engineering program.

---

## Step 1: Register

### Create Your Account

Sign up or Register for SRMJEEE (UG) with basic details:

- Name
- Email ID (will be your username)
- Mobile Number
- State
- Gender
- Password (set by you)

### Confirmation

On successful registration, you will receive a confirmation message on your:
- Registered contact number
- Email ID

Use your Email ID (Username) and Password to log in to your SRMJEEE account.

[Register Now](https://applications.srmist.edu.in/){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

---

## Step 2: Apply for Entrance Exam

### Take SRMJEEE (UG)

After registration, you'll need to:

1. **Complete the application form** with all required details
2. **Upload necessary documents**:
   - Recent photograph
   - Signature
   - 10th & 12th mark sheets
   - Other relevant certificates

3. **Pay the application fee**
4. **Attempt the entrance exam** covering:
   - Physics
   - Chemistry
   - Mathematics
   - English
   - Aptitude

### Exam Pattern

| **Subject** | **Questions** | **Duration** |
|------------|---------------|--------------|
| Physics | 35 questions | 2.5 hours |
| Chemistry | 35 questions | (Total) |
| Mathematics | 35 questions | |
| English & Aptitude | 20 questions | |

---

## Step 3: Admission

### Based on Entrance Results

After the SRMJEEE (UG) results are announced:

1. **Check your results** using your login credentials
2. **Attend counseling** (if shortlisted)
3. **Select your preferred program** (B.Tech EEE)
4. **Pay the admission fee** to confirm your seat
5. **Submit original documents** for verification

### Document Verification

Bring the following original documents for verification:

- 10th Standard Mark Sheet & Certificate
- 12th Standard Mark Sheet & Certificate
- Transfer Certificate (TC)
- Community Certificate (if applicable)
- Income Certificate (for scholarship claims)
- SRMJEEE Rank Card
- Identity Proof (Aadhar Card/Passport)
- Photographs (passport size)

---

## Alternative Admission Routes

### Direct Admission

SRMIST offers **direct admission and scholarships** to students with exceptional talent:

#### Eligible for Direct Admission:
- First rank holders from all central and state boards in India
- Top 10,000 rankers in IIT JEE
- Top rankers in each district of Tamil Nadu
- Exemplary sports persons at National and International levels

### International Students

NRIs and international students who have not taken SRMJEEE (UG) should:

1. Apply through the **International Student Category**
2. Visit: [International Admissions](https://intlapplications.srmist.edu.in/)
3. Follow the specific guidelines for international applicants
