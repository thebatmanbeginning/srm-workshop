## Key Course Highlights

### Core Electrical Engineering Subjects

**Year 1 & 2 Foundation:**
- Circuit Analysis
- Electromagnetic Theory
- Digital System Design
- Electrical Machines (I & II)
- Analog Electronics
- Measurements & Instrumentation

**Year 3 & 4 Advanced:**
- Control Systems
- Power Electronics
- Power System Analysis
- Power System Protection
- Microcontrollers
- Power System Operation and Control

### Mathematical Foundation
- Calculus and Linear Algebra
- Advanced Calculus and Complex Analysis
- Transforms and Boundary Value Problems
- Numerical Methods
- Probability and Statistics
- Discrete Mathematics

### Practical Training
- **Industrial Training**: Two phases in Semesters V & VI
- **Seminars**: Present technical topics
- **MOOC Courses**: Online learning from global platforms
- **Minor Project**: Semester VII
- **Major Project/Internship**: Semester VIII

### Professional Development
- Programming for Problem Solving
- Management Principles for Engineers
- Entrepreneurship Management
- Employability Skills and Practices
- Critical and Creative Thinking Skills

---

## Electives

Students have the flexibility to choose from various **Professional Electives** and **Open Electives** to specialize in areas of interest such as:

- Renewable Energy Systems
- Electric Vehicles
- Smart Grids
- Automation and Robotics
- VLSI Design
- Communication Systems
- AI and Machine Learning applications

!!! info "Elective Selection"
    Detailed list of electives will be provided at the beginning of each semester. Students can choose based on their career interests and industry trends.

## Assessment Pattern

Evaluation is based on:
- **Continuous Internal Assessment**: 40%
- **End Semester Examination**: 60%
- **Practical/Lab Work**: Based on performance and viva
- **Project Work**: Continuous evaluation and final presentation
